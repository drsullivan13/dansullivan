## Synthesis Protocol

1. Set thermal unit to medium heat.

2. Fry curry paste until fragrant (3 min).

3. Mix in peanut butter and coconut milk.

4. Add potatoes. Cook covered for 20 minutes.

5. Stir in tamarind paste.

6. Top with roasted peanuts before serving.

## Mission Notes

This high-protein variant is recommended for crew members engaged in EVA activities or resistance training protocols. Peanut-based compounds support muscle protein synthesis.
