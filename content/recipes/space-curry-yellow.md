## Synthesis Protocol

1. Heat wok to 380¬∞F.

2. Dissolve curry paste in small amount of oil.

3. Pour coconut milk. Stir until homogeneous.

4. Add sweet potato. Cook for 8 minutes.

5. Add cauliflower and turmeric. Simmer 12 minutes.

6. Finish with fresh cilantro.

## Mission Notes

The curcumin in turmeric provides documented anti-inflammatory benefits, reducing joint stress during prolonged exposure to microgravity conditions.
