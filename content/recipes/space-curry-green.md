## Synthesis Protocol

1. Activate thermal chamber to medium-high heat.

2. Introduce green curry paste. Agitate for 90 seconds.

3. Add coconut cream gradually while stirring.

4. Insert vegetables in order of density: eggplant, then beans.

5. Add chickpeas. Simmer for 18 minutes.

6. Garnish with lime leaves. Serve immediately.

## Mission Notes

Green variant recommended for crew members with sensitivity to high-capsaicin compounds. Equally effective for energy maintenance with reduced gastric stress.
